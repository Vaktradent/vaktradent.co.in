·Basic Implantology·

				
The Implant Mini Residency is designed for dentists of all specialties who have limited 
or no experience with dental implants. Attendees will learn the theoretical basics and 
practical procedures for simple, single implant placement and restoration.


·Course Highlights·


Upon successful completion of the course participants will be able to:
1. Determine clinical scenarios and appropriate candidates for implant procedures.
2. Treatment planning.
3. Surgical procedures and concepts.
4. Implant selection.
5. Prosthetic rehabilitation & complete prosthetic set – up.

The basic implant program is a 6 month program divided into four modules 
covering twelve contacts.


·Introduction to basic implantalogy·

	Fundamental sciences
	Diagnosis, and treatment planning
	Patient evaluation
	Detailed radiographic evaluation
	Preimplant prosthetic planning, surgical stents
	Hands on on models. Introduction to armamentarium
	Clinical cases treatment planning


·Surgical Phase·

	Surgical principles
	Implant placement – patient demonstration. [Live Surgery]
	Videos of implant surgeries ranging from simple to advanced cases
	Placement of implants by participants on their individual patients.


·Prosthetic phase·

	Prosthetic components. Implant impressions
	Prosthetic instrumentation
	Videos of detailed prosthetic procedures.
	Hands on demo
	Patient demonstration
	Patient work by participants.


·Graduation·

	Delivery of the prosthesis and interactive session
	Principles of occlusion in general and in implants
	Clinical biomechanics
	Introduction to various implant systems majorly marketed in India
	Practice management
	Advancements in Implantology.

In this program besides extensive hands on activity, participants get to do a single implant 
placement in patient followed by it’s prosthetic loading. Ample reading and reference material 
is provided along with the lecture.Learn all the aspects of implant dentistry form a multitude 
of faculty members comprising of a prosthodontist, an oral and maxillofacial surgeon and a 
periodontist, who will be present throughout to guide you.

	Guest lectures from eminent Senior practitioners will be taken in each module.
	Patients will be provided by the institute for implant placement.
	Plus, get assistance for the first 3 implant surgeries at your practice from our faculties.
