·Full Mouth Rehabilitation·

·Clinical Principles and Techniques·



				
A 3 day course that will help you understand and observe the occlusal disease process better, 
it’s a must-do course for all practitioners since comprehensive management of occlusal
aspects is the need of the hour in clinical dentistry. It’s designed to help you identify
and diagnose occlusal problems and also learn the appropriate protocols and armamentarium
to restore mutilated dentitions to their former glory. This course will take you through 
every step involved in dealing with such clinical cases, right from start to finish. 
It’s important to know the contemporary treatment planning while restoring teeth with 
generalized or localized wear and yet being conservative with newer materials and 
	minimal tooth preps. 

At the end of the course each participant will: 

	Learn to identify occlusal problems in the natural dentition
	Stand the functioning of the TMJ and muscles of mastication
	Be proficient in programming a semi adjustable articulator
	Integrate form and function with beauty
	Understand sequencing of treatment in complex rehabilitations
	Draw a road map of treatment plan of complex cases and execute each step as per the 
	current standards of excellence in dentistry
